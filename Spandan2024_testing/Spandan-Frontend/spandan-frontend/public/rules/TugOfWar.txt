Tug of War
● Rules
○Maximum number of players in a team is 7(5+2).
○Playing team - 5 players
○Substitutions - 2 players
○Maximum weight per Playing team is 350kg.
○No gloves or shoes with spikes are allowed(which includes football
studs) Barefoot is allowed.
○Chalk powder will be provided.
○Both teams must pull only after the signal provided by the referee.
○Both teams must have an equal number of players.
○Substitutions are allowed on the condition that the total weight of the
playing team remains below the maximum limit.
● Scoring
○ There will be 3 rounds. Winning any 2 rounds of these 3 rounds will
qualify you to the next round. Otherwise you are disqualified
